# Sustainable AI

As DSIT accelerates AI-assisted engineering adoption across government through the AI Engineering Lab programme, environmental sustainability must be a core consideration.

These documents outline DSIT's approach to sustainable AI, its environmental impact, and best practice to achieve a net benefit.
